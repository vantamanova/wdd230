Membership levels

NP Membership

Description: Designed for non-profit organizations.

Cost: No membership fee.

Benefits:

  • Access to networking events.
  • Free or discounted training programs.
  • Participation in special non-profit focused workshops.
  • Opportunities for collaboration with other non-profit organizations.
  • Access to resources for fundraising and grant opportunities.
  • Visibility through the Chamber's non-profit directory.

Bronze Membership

Description: Entry-level membership for small businesses.

Cost: $1,000 per year.

Benefits:

  • Invitation to exclusive networking events.
  • Access to discounted training and development programs.
  • Listing in the Chamber's online business directory.
  • Discounts on Chamber-sponsored events and conferences.
  • Opportunities to participate in trade missions and exhibitions.
  • Access to market research reports and business intelligence.

Silver Membership

Description: Intermediate membership level suitable for growing businesses.

Cost: $3,000 per year.

Benefits:

  • Priority invitation to high-profile networking events.
  • Enhanced visibility through sponsorships and advertising opportunities.
  • Access to specialized training workshops and seminars.
  • Discounts on Chamber-affiliated services such as document attestation and certification.
  • Access to government liaison services for business support.
  • Preferential rates for international trade missions and business matchmaking services.

Gold Membership

Description: Premium membership tailored for established corporations.

Cost: $10,000 per year.

Benefits:

  • VIP access to exclusive networking events with top industry leaders and government officials.
  • Customized advertising and promotional opportunities through Chamber channels.
  • Priority access to high-demand training programs and executive education courses.
  • Dedicated account manager for personalized assistance and support.
  • Complimentary access to premium services such as legal consultation and market intelligence reports.
  • Elite invitations to international business delegations and VIP receptions.
